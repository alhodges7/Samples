Browsing Resources



To browse the library, just do the following:

1. Logon on to Astra (if you have not already done so).
2. Click the Browse link on the main site menu.
3. The first page of Resources will be displayed. Each Resource has the following information displayed:

The Title. Click the Title link will take you to a detailed description of the book.
The Author.
The Average Rating for the Resource.
The Key Words that are associated with the Resource.
The Number of Available Copies of the Resource.

If you don't want to browse all of the Resources, you can also search for specific resources.
