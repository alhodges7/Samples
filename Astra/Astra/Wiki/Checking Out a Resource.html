Checking Out a Resource



To check out a Resource from the Astra library, just do the following:

1. Logon to Astra (if you have not already done so).
2. Click the Browse link on the main site menu. (Or, can you also directly search for the Resource you want.)
3. Once you find the Resource in the list, click its title. You will be taken to the Resource Summary page for the Resource.
4. Click the Check Out button.
