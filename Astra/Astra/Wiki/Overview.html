Overview

Astra is an on-line library system designed by the .NET practice at Mindtree's U.S. Delivery Center in Gainesville, Florida. It allows for the tracking and sharing of resources such as Books, DVDs, and other materials.

Astra is configured for "Active Directory Login." Therefore to log into Astra, all you have to do is to:

(1) Log into your Mindtree laptop with your MID.

(2) Be connected to the Mindtree domain. (If you are connected locally with an eithernet cable, or wirelessly on Spectrum, or remotely with the VPN, then you are already on the domain.)

(3) Open Internet Explorer 9+ and go to http://e2ms00002:8042/    Just wait a few seconds, and you're automatically logged in!  If it's your first time opening the Astra web page, it will several seconds to register you and create your account.  If it takes more than 5 minutes, please contact an administrator for assistance.

Astra has two kinds of users:

Basic Users

Basic Users browse the resource catalogue and check them out (and in). See the list of Basic User Features for information on how to use the system.

Librarians

Librarians enter new resources, users, and generally maintain the system. See the list of Librarian Features for information on setting-up the Astra system.
