Checking In a Resource



To check in a Resource back into the Astra library, just do the following:

1. Logon to Astra (if you have not already done so).
2. Click the My Check-Outs link on the main site menu. You will be taken to the My Check-Outs page.
3. Find the Resource in the list.
4. Click the Check In button.
