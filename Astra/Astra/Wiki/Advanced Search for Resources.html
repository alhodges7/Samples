Advanced Search for Resources



To perform an Advanced Search for Resources, just do go to the Home page of the Astra site and click on the Advanced Search pane.

Enter any of the following criteria:

Title -- one or more words of the desired Resource's Title.
Author -- all or part of the name of the desired Resource's Author.
ISBN -- the Resource's ISBN (either 10 digit or 13 digit form).


You can also selected one or more applicable Key Words in the Key Words list to further limit the search results.
