Standard Search for Resources by Key Words



To perform a simple (Standard) search for Resources, just do go to the Home page of the Astra site.

In the Standard Search pane, there is a text box for Key Word(s). Just time in all the Key Words that are relevant to the Reources you are looking for. Key Words, in this case, means either one or more words in the title (e.g., "Easy", "Simple") as well as any actual Key Words indicating the target techology (e.g, "Java").

Then click the Search Button.
